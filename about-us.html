Our Journey

A Legacy Built on Trust & Timber

Founded in 1995, Bhat Group of Companies started with a singular vision: to revolutionize the plywood industry in India. What began as a small timber trading unit has today evolved into Duha Plywood, a name synonymous with strength, durability, and innovation.
