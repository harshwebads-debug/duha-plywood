Who We Are

Crafting Legacies Since 1995.

Welcome to Bhat Group of Companies (Duha Plywood). What started as a humble timber workshop has evolved into a global leader in premium architectural solutions. We don't just manufacture plywood; we curate the foundation of your dream spaces.

Our philosophy is simple: Innovation meets Tradition. By combining age-old woodworking techniques with cutting-edge technology, we deliver products that are not only structurally superior but aesthetically breathtaking.
