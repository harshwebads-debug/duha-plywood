Shuttering Ply

Film Faced / BWP Grade

Premium Shuttering Plywood

Duha Shuttering Plywood is specially engineered for heavy-duty construction and concrete formwork applications. Manufactured with high-density hardwood core and bonded with superior Phenol Formaldehyde resin, it ensures maximum strength, load-bearing capacity, and multiple reuses. The film-faced surface provides a smooth concrete finish and excellent water resistance.

  • High Load Bearing Strength
  • Boiling Water Proof (BWP) Grade
  • High-Density Hardwood Core
  • Multiple Reusability Cycles
  • Smooth Film Faced Surface Finish
  • Ideal for Concrete Formwork
